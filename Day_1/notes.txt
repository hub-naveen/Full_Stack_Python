Formatting Tags:

<B> - Makes text bold
<Strong> - Makes text bold with semantic importance
<i> - Makes text italic
<Em> - Makes text italic with emphasis (semantic)
<U> - Underlines text
<del> - Shows deleted/strikethrough text
<br> - Line break (single tag, no closing tag)
<Sup> - Superscript text (raised above baseline)
<Sub> - Subscript text (lowered below baseline)
<Span> - Generic container/holder for inline content (lightweight inline wrapper)
<div> - Generic block-level container for grouping content
<cr> - Invalid tag (did you mean <br> for line break?)
<R> - register under Government
